# Employee Attrition Prediction

## 📌 Overview
Employee attrition refers to employees leaving an organization. High attrition increases hiring costs and affects productivity.  
This project predicts whether an employee is likely to leave the company by analyzing past employee data.

The aim is to help HR teams identify employees at risk early and take steps to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Many organizations struggle to understand why employees leave. By using historical employee information such as job role, salary, experience, and work-life balance, this project helps predict attrition and highlights key factors behind it.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Programming Language: Python  
- Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn  
- Techniques:
  - Data Cleaning  
  - Feature Engineering  
  - Classification Models  
  - Model Evaluation  

---

## 🔍 Approach
1. Collected and cleaned employee data  
2. Analyzed employee details such as role, income, experience, and work-life balance  
3. Built prediction models including:
   - Logistic Regression  
   - Random Forest  
   - XGBoost  
4. Compared model performance to choose the best model  

---

## 📊 Results
- Achieved around 88% accuracy  
- Identified important factors affecting attrition:
  - Monthly income  
  - Job role  
  - Years at company  
  - Work-life balance  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Employees with lower satisfaction and fewer growth opportunities are more likely to leave  
- Data-driven insights can help HR teams make better retention decisions
